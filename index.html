Our Story

In the midst of a pandemic, Janine and Sammuel's craving for takoyaki led to a kitchen experiment. Unable to find it in stores, their family decided to make their own. To their delight, their homemade takoyaki was a hit with friends and family. Encouraged by their success, they started selling their takoyaki in 2020, and thus, Sam's Takolicious was born.

Sam's Takolicious became more than just a food business; it became a symbol of hope and perseverance during challenging times. Today, as customers line up to savor the irresistible flavors of Sam's Takolicious, they are reminded of the journey that brought this culinary delight to life. From a simple craving to a beloved local treasure, Sam's Takolicious continues to serve up happiness, one takoyaki at a time.
